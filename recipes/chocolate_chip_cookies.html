Chewiest Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Grandma's Chocochip Cookies

This is the recipe you've been looking for. The easiest, chewiest, most delicious baked chocolate chip recipe on the internet. When you're finished, you'll feel like Gordon Ramsey. You won't even remember that the only thing you could make up until this point was a bowl of cereal. Your friends and family will be so appreciative, they'll write you in their wills.

Ingedients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsps hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • optional: 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water
  5. Add to batter along with salt
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and (optional) nuts
  7. Drop in large spoonfuls of mixed batter into ungreased pans
  8. Bake for 9-12 minutes in preheated oven until nicely brown on edges
