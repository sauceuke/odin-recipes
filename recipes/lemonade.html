Thirst Quenching Lemonade Recipe

The Best, Most Thirst-Quenching, Homemade Lemonade

This is the recipe you've been looking for. The most delicious lemonade recipe on the internet. When you're finished, you'll feel like Gordon Ramsey. You won't even remember that the only thing you could make up until this point was a bowl of cereal. Your friends and family will be so appreciative, they'll write you in their wills.

Ingedients

  • 8 lemons
  • 64 oz water
  • 1 3/4 cup sugar
  • raw ginger

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and 1 cup (8oz) of water in small sauce pan, stir until sugar dissolves
  2. Roll around lemons on counter to soften, cut lengthwise and squeeze into measuring cups
  3. Discard any seeds
  4. Pour rest of water into pitcher
  5. Combine lemon juice and water/sugar syrup to taste
  6. Slice ginger into small chunks, or grind in blender and pour into pitcher
  7. Add ice
  8. Enjoy!
