Baked Chicken Recipe

Perfect Summer Baked Chicken

This is the recipe you've been looking for. The easiest, crispiest, most delicious baked chicken recipe on the internet. When you're finished, you'll feel like Gordon Ramsey. You won't even remember that the only thing you could make up until this point was a bowl of cereal. Your friends and family will be so appreciative, they'll write you in their wills.

Ingedients

  • 1-3lb of raw chicken breast
  • seasoning of your choice
  • olive oil
  • 2tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Remove chicken from wrapping and put in large bowl
  3. Add seasonings to chicken in bowl
  4. Add olive oil to chicken and rub seasonings into skin
  5. Place chicken on 9in x 11in metal sheet
  6. Place butter slices on top of chicken breasts
  7. When oven is preheated and dings, place sheet in oven
  8. Cook chicken for 35-45 minutes, or until internal temp is 165 degrees
